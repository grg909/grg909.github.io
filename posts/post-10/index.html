Tech Lead给年轻程序员的tips

Summary

  • Don’t try to learn everything. (The industry changes quickly.)
  • All code is garbage. (Don’t let the perfect be the enemy of the good.)
  • Focus on the project and not the technology.
  • Don’t be afraid to learn. (The mental barrier is often more difficult to handle than the software.)

Memo

  • Software is mostly just duct tape and strings
    • 软件就像机械系统一样，主要是装配件的组合
    • 但和制造业不同，虽然有大量轮子（标准件）可用，软件业仍然大量创造新组件，所以会产生很多Garbage code， 需要定期重构
  • More than anything, software is about your willingness to sit down and learn something
    • 足够多的时间块（不是碎片时间）
    • 快速学习，输出prototype是关键
